A guide to breathing

Square breathing, sometimes known as box breathing, is a method of taking slow, deep breaths that aims to heighten concentration and relieve stress.

It comprises the following steps:

  1. Inhale for a count of four seconds
  2. Keep your lungs full for a count of four seconds
  3. Let out all of the air in your lungs for a count of four seconds
  4. Keep your lungs empty for a count of four seconds

The steps are repeated as many times as needed.
